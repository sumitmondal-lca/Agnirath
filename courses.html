Courses

  • Master Class

    Our Master Classes are designed as short-duration, intensive learning experiences that focus on a single advanced topic. Typically lasting a few hours, these sessions are ideal for students, professionals, and enthusiasts who want to quickly gain practical knowledge in specific areas of space technology, robotics, programming, or artificial intelligence. Each Master Class is led by experts from academia or industry who present real-world use cases, demonstrations, and problem-solving approaches. By keeping the sessions highly focused and hands-on, learners walk away with new skills they can immediately apply to their studies, projects, or research. Master Classes are perfect for those who have limited time but want impactful learning in a short span.

  • Certificate Course

    Our Certificate Courses are three-month-long, detail-oriented programs that provide participants with a solid understanding of core concepts and their practical applications. Designed for students and professionals alike, these courses combine classroom learning, assignments, and small projects to ensure strong skill development. Areas of focus include aerospace fundamentals, satellite systems, robotics, AI, and space management. By the end of the program, learners not only earn a recognized certificate but also gain industry-relevant experience that enhances their career prospects. With flexible learning formats, expert instructors, and guided mentorship, our Certificate Courses are a strong stepping stone for those looking to build a foundation in space and technology domains.

  • Diploma Course

    Our Diploma Courses are comprehensive six-month-long programs designed to provide in-depth, syllabus-oriented professional training. These programs include structured modules, lab work, hands-on experiments, industry-standard tools, and real project experience. A key highlight of the Diploma Course is the inclusion of industry visits and field exposure, giving participants direct insights into how concepts are applied in real-world aerospace, defense, and AI projects. The program also integrates mentorship and capstone projects, ensuring students graduate with not only theoretical knowledge but also practical problem-solving skills. A Diploma from Agnirath equips learners with the confidence and technical expertise needed to contribute to industry and research at a professional level.

  • School Level Course

    Our School Level Courses are specially designed STEM programs that nurture curiosity and creativity in young minds. Delivered through workshops and projects, these courses introduce students to the fundamentals of robotics, programming, artificial intelligence, and aerospace in a fun and interactive way. Students learn by building simple robots, coding basic programs, and experimenting with hands-on models that spark their imagination. These courses align with school-level learning but go beyond textbooks by offering real-world applications and problem-solving activities. By engaging students at an early stage, we aim to inspire them to pursue careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, and help them develop the skills required to become the innovators of tomorrow.
